Welcome to Crab Battle, a two-player fighting game.

+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
CONTROLS

Escape  - - - - - Pause/Resume/Go back to the last menu
Backspace - - - - Abort the current game

Player 1 --
    W - - - - - - Jump
    A - - - - - - Go left
    D - - - - - - Go right
    Space Bar - - Punch

Player 2 --
    Up Arrow  - - Jump
    Left Arrow  - Go left
    Right Arrow - Go right
    Enter - - Punch
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

To start a game, choose "New Game" from the main menu by pressing Enter.  A
player select screen will appear, asking you to choose which character you
want to be.  Use your punch button to make your selection.  After both players
have chosen, the game will commence.  Whoever runs out of lives first is the
loser.  Good luck!
